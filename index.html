But what does the data suggest? Developer's living environment trends for looking for a new job is based on the current developer feedbacks. Therefore, I analyze dataset from Stackoverflow’s 2017 Annual Developer Survey, to take a closer look at this question. The survey data covers over 51000 feedbacks from 200 countries and territories. The survey presents multiple aspects of jobs related to software developer and data analytics. There were more than 154 features as a part of the survey.
